Poland's consumer price inflation rose in July after easing in the
previous month, preliminary data from Statistics Poland showed on
Friday.

The consumer price index rose 5.0 percent year-on-year in July,
following a 4.4 percent increase in June.

Prices for fuels for personal transport equipment grew 30.0 percent
yearly in July. Prices for electricity, gas and other fuels, and food
and non-alcoholic beverages rose by 5.3 percent and 3.1 percent,
respectively.

On a month-on-month basis, consumer prices rose 0.4 percent in July.
