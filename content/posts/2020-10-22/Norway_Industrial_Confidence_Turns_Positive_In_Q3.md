Sentiment in the Norwegian industrial sector turned positive in the
third quarter amid improvement in production, employment and demand
expectations, survey data from Statistics Norway showed on Thursday.

The seasonally adjusted industrial confidence indicator rose to +1.7
from -9.6 in the second quarter. The reading entered positive territory
for the first time since the third quarter of 2019.

The index covers both manufacturing and mining and quarrying sectors.

The diffusion indexes for the outlook on production, employment,
domestic orders and export demand increased in the third quarter, yet
remained below the 50-mark, suggesting contraction.
