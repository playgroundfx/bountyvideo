Italy's industrial production rose at a softer pace in July, data
published by the statistical office Istat showed on Friday.

Industrial output rose 0.8 percent monthly in July, after a 1.1 percent
growth in June. Economists had expected a 0.1 percent rise.

Capital output grew 1.9 percent and intermediate goods output increased
1.4 percent. Consumer goods rose 0.9 percent, while energy goods
declined 1.5 percent.

On a yearly basis, industrial production growth eased to 7.0 percent in
July from 13.8 percent in the previous month.
