Dutch industrial production increased at a softer pace in August,
figures from the Central Bureau of Statistics showed on Friday.

Industrial production grew 9.8 percent year-on-year in August, after a
13.7 percent rise in July. Output increased for the sixth month in a
row.

More than two-thirds of all [business][1] classes in industry produced
more in August. Production in the machine industry grew the most, by
52.6 percent.

On a seasonally and working-day adjusted basis, industrial production
rose 1.9 percent in August.
