The recovery from the [coronavirus][1] crisis is set to be incomplete
and transformational, European Central Bank President Christine Lagarde
said on Friday.

At an online event, she said the recovery will be restrained.

"We probably have passed the lowest point, and I say that with some
trepidation," Lagarde said.

She observed that the hardest hit industries, namely airlines,
hospitality and [entertainment][2] will emerge from the current
situation in a different shape.

"We need to be extremely attentive to those that are most vulnerable,"
said Lagarde.
