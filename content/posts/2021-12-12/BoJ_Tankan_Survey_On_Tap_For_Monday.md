The Bank of Japan will on Monday release its quarterly Tankan Survey of
[business][1] sentiment, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific
economic activity.

In the previous survey, the large manufacturers index had a score of +18
with an outlook of +14, while the large non-manufacturing index had a
score of +2 with an outlook of +3. All-industry capex was seen higher by
10.1 percent.

Japan also will see October results for core machine orders; in
September, orders were flat on month and up 12.5 percent on year.

Hong Kong will provide Q3 data for industrial production; in the three
months prior, output advanced 5.6 percent on year.
