Singapore will on Wednesday release November numbers for import, export
and producer prices, setting the pace for a light day in Asia-Pacific
economic activity.

In October, import prices rose 20.0 percent on year, while export prices
jumped an annual 20.7 percent and producer prices spiked 25.4 percent.

Thailand will provide November figures for industrial production, with
forecasts suggesting an increase of 2.7 percent on year - slowing from
2.91 percent in October.

The Philippines will see November data for producer prices; in October,
prices were up 0.1 percent on year.
