Portugal's retail sales continued to decline in January, figures from
Statistics Portugal showed on Monday.

Retail sales fell 10.9 percent year-on-year in January, following an 4.2
percent decrease in December. Sales fell for the fourth straight month.

Sales of non-food products decreased 19.2 percent annually in January
and those of food products rose 0.1 percent.

On a month-on-month basis, retail sales fell 4.8 percent in January,
following a 0.1 percent decrease in the preceding month. Sales declined
for the third consecutive month.
