product, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic activity. In
the previous reading, GDP was up 0.2 percent on quarter and down 3.0
percent on year.

China will provide April figures for foreign direct investment; in
March, FDI surged 39.9 percent on year.

New Zealand will see April numbers for the Performance of Manufacturing
Index from BusinessNZ; in March, the index score was 63.6.
