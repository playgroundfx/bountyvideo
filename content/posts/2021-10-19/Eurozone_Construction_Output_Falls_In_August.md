Eurozone construction output declined in August after rising for the
first time in four months in July, data from Eurostat showed on Tuesday.

Construction output declined 1.3 percent month-on-month in August, in
contrast to a 0.1 percent rise in July.

Building output decreased 1.3 percent and civil engineering fell 1.9
percent.

On a yearly basis, construction output was down 1.6 percent after rising
3.5 percent in July.

Construction output in the EU27 fell 1.8 percent month-on-month and by 1
percent from the last year.
