Dutch industrial production declined in November, albeit at a softer
pace, figures from the Central Bureau of Statistics showed on Friday.

Industrial production decreased 2.5 percent year-on-year in November,
following a 3.6 percent fall in October.

Half of the all [business][1] classes in industry produced more in
November. Production in the chemical industry grew the most, by 11.1
percent.

Meanwhile, production in repair and installation machines declined 21.8
percent.

On a seasonally and working-day adjusted basis, industrial production
fell 0.2 percent in November.
