India's industrial production grew less than expected in November, data
published by the statistics ministry revealed on Wednesday.

Industrial production gained 1.4 percent year-on-year in November.
Output was forecast to grow 3 percent after climbing 4 percent in
October. In the same period last year, industrial output was down 1.6
percent.

Mining output grew 5 percent and production of electricity gained 2.1
percent. Manufacturing output posted a moderate growth of 0.9 percent.

During April to November, industrial production surged 17.4 percent from
the previous year.
