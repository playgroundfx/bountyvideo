Producer prices in South Korea were down 0.7 percent on month in April,
the Bank of Korea said on Thursday - following the 0.9 percent drop in
March.

Individually, prices for agricultural, forestry and marine products
added 0.2 percent on month, while manufacturing products dropped 1.5
percent, utilities eased 0.1 percent and services were flat.

On a yearly basis, producer prices fell 1.5 percent after sliding 0.6
percent in the previous month.

The domestic supply price index fell 2.1 percent on month in April and
2.9 percent on year.
