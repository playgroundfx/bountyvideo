## [Walmart Enables Crypto Cashback On Purchases ][118]

  
  
Retail giant Walmart has enabled cashback in cryptocurrencies for their
shopping customers in partnership with [blockchain](https://www.letsplayfx.com/blog/trade-forex-with-bitcoin/)-powered gamified
shopping loyalty platform StormX. Users of the platform can earn up to 4
percent in crypto cashback while shopping online from Walmart stores.
Diamond members of StormX are eligible to receive up to 14 percent on
most purchases.
