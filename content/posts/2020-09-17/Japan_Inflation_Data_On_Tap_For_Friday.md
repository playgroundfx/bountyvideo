Japan will on Friday release August numbers for consumer prices,
highlighting a very light day for Asia-Pacific economic activity.

Overall inflation is tipped to slow to 0.1 percent on year from 0.3
percent in July. On a monthly basis, inflation is pegged at -0.4 percent
following the flat reading in the previous month. Core CPI was up 0.4
percent on year in July.
