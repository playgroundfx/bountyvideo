Consumer prices in South Korea were up 3.7 percent on year in February,
Statistics Korea said on Friday.

That was above expectations for 3.5 percent and up from 3.6 percent in
the previous month.

On a monthly basis, inflation was up 0.6 percent - unchanged from the
January reading and beating forecasts for an increase of 0.4 percent.
