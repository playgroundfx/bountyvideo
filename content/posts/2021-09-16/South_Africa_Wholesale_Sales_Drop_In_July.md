South Africa's wholesale sales dropped for the first time in five months
in July, data from Statistics South Africa showed on Thursday.

Wholesale sales grew an unadjusted 2.0 percent year-on-year in July,
after a 10.9 percent rise in June.

On a monthly basis, wholesale sales declined a seasonally adjusted 3.8
percent in July, after a 4.8 percent fall in the previous month.

For the three months ended in July, wholesale sales rose 1.6 percent,
after a 2.1 percent increase in the previous three months ended in June.
