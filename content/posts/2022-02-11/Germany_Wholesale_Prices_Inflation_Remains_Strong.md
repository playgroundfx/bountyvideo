Germany's wholesale prices continued to increase sharply in January,
data from Destatis showed on Friday.

Wholesale prices increased 16.2 percent year-on-year in January, after a
16.1 percent rise in December. In November, prices grew 16.6 percent.

On a monthly basis, wholesale prices rose 2.3 percent in January,
following a 0.2 percent gain in the previous month.

There was a sharp increase in prices of many raw materials and
intermediate products in January, Destatis said.

Wholesale prices of petroleum products grew 41.9 percent yearly in
January.
