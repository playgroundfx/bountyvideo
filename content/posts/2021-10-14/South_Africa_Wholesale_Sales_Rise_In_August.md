South Africa's wholesale sales rose in August after falling in the
previous month, data from Statistics South Africa showed on Thursday.

Wholesale sales grew an unadjusted 4.3 percent year-on-year in August,
after a 1.8 percent drop in July.

On a monthly basis, wholesale sales gained a seasonally adjusted 2.7
percent in August, after a 2.9 percent fall in the previous month.

For the three months ended in August, wholesale sales fell 2.0 percent,
after a 1.8 percent increase in the previous three months ended in July.
