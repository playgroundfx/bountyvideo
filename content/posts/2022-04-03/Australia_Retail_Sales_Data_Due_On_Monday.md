Australia will on Monday release final February figures for retail
sales, headlining a light day for Asia-Pacific economic activity. Sales
are expected to rise 1.8 percent on month, accelerating from 1.6 percent
in January.

Australia also will see March job ads data from ANZ; in February, job
ads jumped 8.4 percent on month.

Finally, the [markets][1] in Taiwan and China are closed on Monday, for
Children's Day and the Qingming Festival, respectively.
