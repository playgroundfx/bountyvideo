Poland's jobless rate decreased marginally in April, figures from the
statistical office showed on Wednesday.

The unemployment rate fell to 6.3 percent in April from 6.4 percent in
March. This was in line with economists' expectation.

In the same month last year, the unemployment rate was 5.8 percent.

The newly registered unemployed persons increased to 84,600 in April
from 102,600 in the previous month.

The number of youth unemployed persons, which is applied to below 25 age
group, rose to 124,500 in April from 131,100 in the previous month.
