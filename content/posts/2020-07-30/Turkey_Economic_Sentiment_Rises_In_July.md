Turkey's economic confidence increased in July, figures from the Turkish
Statistical Institute showed on Thursday.

The economic confidence index increased to 82.2 in July from 73.5 in
June.

The consumer confidence index fell to 60.9 in July from 62.6 in the
previous month.

The measure of manufacturing industry morale rose to 99.4 in July and
the confidence index for services increased to 66.7.

The confidence measures for retail trade increased to 94.6 in July, and
that for construction sector rose to 87.0.
