Romania's industrial production rose in August, data from the National
Institute of Statistics showed on Wednesday.

Industrial production rose a seasonally adjusted 3.9 percent month-on-
month in August.

Manufacturing output rose 4.2 percent monthly in August.

Meanwhile, production in mining and quarrying decreased 3.7 percent and
electricity fell 1.2 percent.

On a yearly basis, industrial production decreased a working-day
adjusted 5.1 percent in August.

On an unadjusted basis, industrial production declined 10.5 percent
monthly in August and fell 4.3 percent from a year ago.
