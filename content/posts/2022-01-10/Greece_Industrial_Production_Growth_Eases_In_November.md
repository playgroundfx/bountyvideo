Greece industrial production grew at a moderate pace in November after
posting a sharp growth in October, the Hellenic Statistical Authority
reported Monday.

Industrial production increased 8.0 percent on a yearly basis in
November, following October's sharp 16.5 percent expansion.

Data showed that manufacturing grew 9.1 percent and mining and quarrying
output moved up 5.9 percent. Electricity output was up 4.7 percent and
water supply output by 2.9 percent.

On a monthly basis, industrial output dropped 1.8 percent, much slower
than the 3.3 percent decrease posted in October.
