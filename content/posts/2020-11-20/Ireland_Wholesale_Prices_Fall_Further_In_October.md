Ireland's wholesale prices declined further in October, data from the
Central Statistics Office showed on Friday.

Wholesale prices decreased 5.1 percent annually in October, following a
12.4 percent decline in September.

On a monthly basis, wholesale prices rose 0.9 percent in October,
following a 0.5 percent increase in the previous month.

Prices for export sales increased by 0.8 percent monthly in October and
fell 5.3 percent from a year ago.

Prices for home sales remained unchanged from September and declined 1.2
percent from the previous year.
