The euro area unemployment rate dropped marginally in August, data from
Eurostat revealed on Thursday.

The jobless rate fell to 7.5 percent in August, in line with
expectations, from 7.6 percent in July. In the same period last year,
the unemployment rate was 8.6 percent.

The number of people out of work decreased 261,000 to 12.162 million in
August. Compared to last year, unemployment was down 1.861 million.

The youth unemployment rate came in at 16.4 percent in August versus
16.7 percent in July.

The overall unemployment rate in the EU dropped to 6.8 percent in August
from 6.9 percent in July.
