Switzerland's retail sales increased in November, preliminary data from
the Federal Statistical Office showed on Thursday.

Retail sales adjusted for sales days and holidays rose by 1.7 percent
from a year ago.

Sales of food, beverages and tobacco gained 7.7 percent yearly in
November, while those of non-food sector fell 0.4 percent.

On a monthly basis, seasonally adjusted retail sales fell 2.0 percent in
November.

In nominal [terms](https://www.fintechee.com/terms/), retail sales grew 1.0 percent annually in November and
decreased 2.4 percent from a month ago.
