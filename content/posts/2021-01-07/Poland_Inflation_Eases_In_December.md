Poland's consumer price inflation eased for the third straight month in
December, preliminary data from Statistics Poland showed on Thursday.

The consumer price index rose 2.3 percent year-on-year in December,
after a 3.0 percent increase in November. Economists had expected a 2.6
percent rise.

Prices for food and non-alcoholic beverages grew 0.7 percent yearly in
December and those of electricity, gas and other fuels gained by 4.6
percent.

Meanwhile, prices of fuels for transport declined 9.4 percent.

On a month-on-month basis, consumer prices rose 0.1 percent in December.
