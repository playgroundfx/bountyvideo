Denmark's retail sales grew for the first time in four months in
October, figures from Statistics Denmark showed on Thursday.

Retail sales rose a seasonally adjusted 1.7 percent month-on-month in
October, after a 1.2 percent declined in September.

Sales of clothing and other goods increased 3.2 percent monthly in
October. Sales of other consumables gained 1.9 percent and those sales
of food and grocery rose 1.1 percent.

On an annual basis, retail sales decreased 2.5 percent in October, after
a 3.0 percent growth in the previous month.
