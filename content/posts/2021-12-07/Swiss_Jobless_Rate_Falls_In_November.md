Switzerland's jobless rate decreased November, data from the State
Secretariat for Economic Affairs showed on Tuesday.

The jobless rate fell a seasonally to 2.5 percent in November from 2.7
percent in October.

On an unadjusted basis, the unemployment rate was 2.5 percent in
November, same as in the previous month.

The number of registered unemployed declined to 116,244 in November from
116,733 in the preceding month.

The unemployment rate among the youth aged between 15 and 24, decreased
to 2.0 percent in November from 2.1 percent in the prior month.
