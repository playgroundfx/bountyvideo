Romania's industrial production increased in April, data from the
National Institute of Statistics showed on Monday.

Industrial production rose a seasonally adjusted 2.7 percent month-on-
month in April.

Manufacturing output accelerated 6.2 percent monthly in April.

Production in mining and quarrying grew 10.7 percent and energy gained
2.2 percent.

On a yearly basis, industrial production surged a working-day adjusted
63.2 percent in April.

On an unadjusted basis, industrial production declined 5.9 percent
monthly in April and grew 68.0 percent from a year ago.
