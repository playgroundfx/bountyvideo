Denmark's [business][1] confidence improved in August, figures from
Statistics Denmark showed on Friday.

The business confidence rose to 84.9 in August from 82.0 in July.

Industrial confidence deteriorated to -10 in August from -7 in the
previous month.

Confidence index in the construction sector rose to -10 in August from
-13 in the previous month.

The morale in the services sector rose to -17 from -27 in the preceding
month.

The retail sector deteriorated to 5 in August from 10 in the prior
month.
