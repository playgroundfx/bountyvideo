Switzerland's retail sales decreased in July, preliminary data from the
Federal Statistical Office showed on Thursday.

Retail sales adjusted for sales days and holidays declined 2.6 percent
year-on-year in July.

On a monthly basis, seasonally adjusted retail sales declined 2.8
percent in July.

Sales of food, beverages and tobacco fell 4.3 percent yearly in July and
sales of non-food decreased 1.5 percent, the agency said.

In nominal [terms](https://www.fintechee.com/terms/), retail sales decreased 2.9 percent annually in July
and fell 2.6 percent from a month ago.
