Unemployment in Spain rose in October after falling in the previous
month, figures from the labor ministry showed on Wednesday.

The number of unemployed grew by 1.31 percent or by 49,558 persons after
a falling by 26,329 persons in September.

The increase was the second-lowest in the past 13 years, the ministry
said. In the same month last year, joblessness grew by 97,948 persons.

On a seasonally adjusted basis, registered unemployment fell by 46,014
persons.

The total number of unemployed was 3.83 million.
