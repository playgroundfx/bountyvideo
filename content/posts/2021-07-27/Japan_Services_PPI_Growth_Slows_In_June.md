Japan's services producer prices grew at a slower pace in June, the Bank
of Japan reported on Tuesday.

The services producer price index rose 1.4 percent year-on-year in June,
slightly slower than the 1.5 percent increase seen in May.

On a monthly basis, the services PPI edged up 0.1 percent, offsetting a
0.1 percent drop in the previous month.

Excluding international transportations, producer price inflation eased
to 1.1 percent year-on-year from 1.4 percent in May. On month, the
services PPI gained 0.1 percent.
