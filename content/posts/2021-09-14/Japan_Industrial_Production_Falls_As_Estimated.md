Japan's industrial production declined in July, as initially estimated,
data from the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry said on Monday.

Industrial production fell a seasonally adjusted 1.5 percent monthly in
July, as estimated.

Shipments decreased 0.3 percent on month in July and inventories fell
0.7 percent in .

Meanwhile, the inventory ratio grew 1.0 percent.

On a yearly basis, industrial production accelerated 11.6 percent in
July, as initially estimated.

Capacity utilization declined 3.4 percent monthly in July and gained
14.1 percent from a year ago.
