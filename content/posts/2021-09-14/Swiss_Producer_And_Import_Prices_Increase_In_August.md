Switzerland's producer and import prices increased in August, data from
the Federal Statistical Office showed on Tuesday.

Producer and import prices rose 4.4 percent year-on-year in August.

The producer price index increased 2.9 percent annually in August and
import prices accelerated 7.6 percent.

On a monthly basis, producer and import prices increased 0.7 percent in
August.

Prices for chemical products and pharmaceutical products were higher in
August, the agency said.

Domestic sale prices grew 2.6 percent yearly in August and rose 0.2
percent from a month ago.
