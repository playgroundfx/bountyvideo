Japan will on Monday see final August figures for its leading and
coincident indexes, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic
activity. The coincident index is tipped to come in at 79.4, up from
78.3 - while the leading index is pegged at 88.8, up from 86.7.

Singapore will release September numbers for industrial production; in
August, production was up 13.9 percent on month and 13.7 percent on
year.

Finally, the [markets][1] in Hong Kong (Chung Yeung Festival) and New
Zealand (Labor day) are closed on Monday and will re-open on Tuesday.
