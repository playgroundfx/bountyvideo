The Philippines posted a merchandise trade deficit of $1.776 billion in
October, the statistics office said on Thursday.

That follows the $1.707 billion shortfall in the previous month.

Exports were down 2.2 percent on year after rising 2.2 percent in
September.

Imports plummeted an annual 19.5 percent after slipping 16.5 percent a
month earlier.
