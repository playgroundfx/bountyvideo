Denmark retail sales rose for the first time in four months in February,
figures from the Statistics Denmark showed on Friday.

Retail sales grew a seasonally adjusted 2.9 percent month-on-month in
February, after a 4.9 percent decrease in January.

Sales of clothing and other goods accelerated 17.9 percent monthly in
February. Sales of food and grocery gained 1.1 percent and those of
other consumables increased 3.5 percent.

On an annual basis, retail sales decreased 4.9 percent in February,
following a 7.5 percent fall in the previous month.
