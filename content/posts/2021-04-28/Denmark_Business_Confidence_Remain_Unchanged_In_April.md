Denmark's [business][1] confidence remained unchanged in April, figures
from Statistics Denmark showed on Wednesday.

The industrial confidence index remained unchanged at 1 in April.

The overall business confidence increased to 106.2 in April from 101.3
in the previous month.

The confidence index in the construction sector grew to 1 in April from
-1 in the prior month.

The morale in the services sector gained to 1 in April from -4 in the
preceding month.

The retail sector confidence index improved to 5 in April from -4 in the
previous month.
