and trade balance, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic
activity.

In November, imports were up 10.0 percent on month and exports gained an
annual 3.0 percent for a trade surplus of A$5.022 billion.

New Zealand will provide December figures for building permits; in
November, permits were up 1.2 percent on month.

Thailand will see January results for its consumer confidence index; in
December, the index score was 50.1.
