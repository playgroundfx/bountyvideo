Denmark's [business][1] confidence decrease in February, figures from
Statistics Denmark showed on Thursday.

The industrial confidence index fell to -4 in February from to -3 in
January.

The overall business confidence decreased to 93.5 in February from 94.9
in the previous month.

The confidence index in the construction sector fell to -6 in February
from -5 in the prior month.

The morale in the services sector declined to -12 in February from -11
in the preceding month.

The retail sector confidence index weakened to -11 in February from -1
in the previous month.
