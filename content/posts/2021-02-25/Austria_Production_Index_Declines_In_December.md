Austria's production index dropped in December, as industrial production
and construction output declined, data from Statistics Austria showed on
Thursday.

The production index decreased 5.1 percent year-on-year in December,
after a 0.4 percent growth in November.

Industrial production declined 3.0 percent annually in December and
construction output decreased 10.3 percent.

On a month-on-month basis, the production index fell 5.6 percent in
December, following a 0.3 percent decline in the prior month.

On a monthly basis, industrial production declined 3.9 percent in
December and construction output decreased 11.0 percent.
