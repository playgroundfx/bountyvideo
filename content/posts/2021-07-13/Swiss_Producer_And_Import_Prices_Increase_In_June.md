Switzerland's producer and import prices increased in June, data from
the Federal Statistical Office showed on Tuesday.

Producer and import prices rose 2.9 percent year-on-year in June.

The producer price index increased 1.7 percent annually in June and
import prices accelerated 5.6 percent.

On a monthly basis, producer and import prices increased 0.3 percent in
June.

Prices for basic metals and semi-finished metal products, scrap and
petroleum products were higher in June, the agency said.

Domestic sale prices grew 2.0 percent yearly in June and rose 0.2
percent from a month ago.
