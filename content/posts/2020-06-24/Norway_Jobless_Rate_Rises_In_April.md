Norway's jobless rate rose in April, the labor force survey data from
Statistics Norway showed on Wednesday.

The jobless rate rose to 4.2 percent in April from 3.7 percent in
January. Economists had expected a rate of 3.8 percent.

The unemployment rate for April indicates the average for March to May
and that for January, reflects the average for December to February.

The unemployment rate was 3.6 percent in March.

The number of unemployed persons decreased to 118,000 in April from
106,000 in the three months to January.
