Unemployment data from the UK is due on Thursday, headlining a light day
for the European economic [news](https://www.letsplayfx.com/blog/forex-news-website/).

At 2.00 am ET, the Office for National Statistics releases UK
unemployment data. The jobless rate is seen unchanged at 4.7 percent in
three months to May.

At 4.00 am ET, Italy's Istat publishes final consumer price data for
June. Consumer price inflation is seen unchanged at 1.3 percent and in
line with preliminary estimate.  
  
In the meantime, Poland's final consumer price data is due for June.
According to flash estimate, consumer prices grew 4.4 percent annually
after rising 4.7 percent in May.

Half an hour later, the Bank of England publishes Credit Conditions
survey results.
