Austria's trade deficit widened in December, data from Statistics
Austria showed on Thursday.

The trade deficit increased to EUR 303.981 million in December from EUR
152.635 million in November.

Exports gained 1.8 percent yearly in December, following a 2.4 percent
rise in November.

Imports increased 1.7 percent annually in December, following a 0.9
percent increase in the previous month.

In 2020, trade deficit decreased to EUR 2.27 billion from EUR 4.315
billion in 2019. Exports and imports declined 7.5 percent and 8.6
percent, respectively.
