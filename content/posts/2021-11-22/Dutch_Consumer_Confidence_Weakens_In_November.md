The Netherlands' consumer confidence weakened in November, data from the
Central Bureau of Statistics showed on Monday.

The consumer confidence index fell to-19 in November from -10 in
October. The score was below the 20-year average of -8 points.

Among components, the economic climate index decreased to -28 in
November from -14 in October. The assessment of the future economic
climate and consumers' opinion on the past economic situation were more
negative.

The indicator for willingness to buy fell to -14 in November from -7 in
the previous month.
