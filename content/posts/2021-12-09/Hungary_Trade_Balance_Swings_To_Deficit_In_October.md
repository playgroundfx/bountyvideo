Hungary's trade balance swung to a deficit in October, preliminary data
from the Hungarian Central Statistical Office showed on Thursday.

The trade balance registered a deficit of EUR 335 million in October
versus a surplus of EUR 920 million in the same month last year.
Economists had expected a deficit of EUR 62.0 million.

In September, the trade deficit was EUR 128 million.

Exports rose 0.9 percent year-on-year in October, after remaining
unchanged in September.

Imports increased 14.4 percent annually in October, after a 14.3 percent
rise in the preceding month.
