Hong Kong will on Monday release February figures for imports, exports
and trade balance, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic
activity.

In January, imports were up 9.6 percent on year and exports jumped an
annual 18.4 percent, resulting in a HKD6.6 billion trade surplus.

Taiwan will see March results for its consumer confidence index; in
February, the index score was 73.19.

The Philippines also will provide Q1 results for its consumer confidence
index; the reading in the three months prior was -24.0.
