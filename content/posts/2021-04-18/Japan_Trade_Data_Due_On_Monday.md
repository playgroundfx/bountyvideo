Japan will on Monday release March figures for imports, exports and
trade balance, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic
activity. In February, imports were up 11.8 percent on year and exports
were down an annual 4.5 percent for a trade surplus of 217.4 billion
yen.

Japan also will see final February figures for industrial production;
the previous reading had industrial production up 4.3 percent on month
and down 5.2 percent on year.

New Zealand will see March results for the Performance of Service Index
form BusinessNZ; in February, the index score was 49.1.
