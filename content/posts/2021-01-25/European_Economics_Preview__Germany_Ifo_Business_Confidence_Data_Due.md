day for the European economic [news](https://www.letsplayfx.com/blog/forex-news-website/).

industrial production from Austria are due.

At 3.45 am ET, European Central Bank President Christine Lagarde is set
to speak at a conference in Frankfurt.

At 4.00 am ET, Germany ifo business confidence survey results are due.
Economists forecast the business sentiment index to fall to 91.8 in
January from 92.1 in the previous month.

In the meantime, industrial production data is due from Poland. Output
is seen rising 8.7 percent on year in December, faster than the 5.4
percent rise seen in November.
