Austria's production index rose in November, as industrial production
increased, data from Statistics Austria showed on Monday.

The production index increased 0.1 percent year-on-year in November,
after remaining unchanged in October.

Industrial production rose 0.7 percent annually in November, while
construction output decreased 1.8 percent.

On a month-on-month basis, the production index fell 0.8 percent in
November, after a 1.6 percent gain in the prior month.

On a monthly basis, industrial production declined 1.6 percent in
November and construction output rose 2.2 percent.
