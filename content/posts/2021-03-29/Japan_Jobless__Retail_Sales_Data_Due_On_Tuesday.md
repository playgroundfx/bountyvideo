Japan will on Tuesday release February numbers for unemployment and
retail sales, highlighting a light day for Asia-Pacific economic
activity.

The jobless rate is expected to rise to 3.0 percent from 2.9 percent in
January. Retail sales were down 0.5 percent on month and 2.4 percent on
year in January.

New Zealand will see February data for building permits; in January,
permits were up 2.1 percent on month.

Hong Kong will see February figures for retail sales; in January, retail
sales were down 14.5 percent on year.
